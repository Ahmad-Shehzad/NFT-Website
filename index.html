About Us

Welcome to iime. We are digital artists that create unique and exciting collections of NFTs, available on Opensea. These collections are incredibly diverse and ground breaking. We started with the Spud Club, which was a 10 piece collection of super cool spuds. Then, we went in a completely different direction and released Shapestract, a collection of 10,000 abstract pieces. This collection yielded our very first sale, #00016. Most recently, we have launched our most exciting collection to date, Sheep Shop. This flock of sheep were abandoned by their farmer, and we are desperately trying to find them new homes, quite a task when there are 10,000 of them. We look forward to bringing you more collections as soon as December.

How to Buy

You can purchase one of our NFTs by either clicking the link to our Opensea account or by clicking on one of the collections above, taking you straight to the relevant collection.Our NFTs are sold on the Polygon blockchain, this means there are no gas fees for you to pay, just the cost of the NFT. You may wonder, "Why not use the Ethereum Blockchain?", well besides the expense of gas fees, using the Ethereum blockchain has a big impact on our carbon footprint hence, we opt to use Polygon.
